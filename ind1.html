Our Goal

Making research accessible to everyone

100%Web-based
0Coding required

Our mission is to empower businesses and researchers to conduct high-quality, ethical chatbot user experiments — without needing deep technical expertise. TechnoDevelop provides an integrated system to configure experiments, build chatbot interfaces, and gather user insights efficiently.

  • ✓ Configure studies with demographic filters and randomization
  • ✓ Build chatbot interfaces using pre-defined templates
  • ✓ Collect validated UX survey data automatically

We envision a future where every company can test, refine, and deploy chatbots based on real user data. TechnoDevelop bridges research and practice — enabling accessible, data-driven chatbot design through structured experimentation and modern AI integration.

  • ✓ Real user data driving every design decision
  • ✓ Research-grade rigor for everyday product teams
  • ✓ Continuous learning through structured experiments
